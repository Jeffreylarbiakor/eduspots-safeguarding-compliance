Network-wide training compliance for the Keeping Spots Safe programme. Figures update automatically as training records change.

Scope: training compliance only This tool tracks training dates and renewal status. It does not track safeguarding incidents, concerns, allegations, or any Catalyst-conduct information — and is not designed to. For actual safeguarding concerns, follow EduSpots' Keeping Spots Safe policy and contact the designated leads directly.

Renewal Reminders

Every Catalyst who is expiring soon or overdue. Use the acknowledge button to log that the relevant RC has been notified and owns follow-up.

Human handoff — how this works Acknowledging a reminder logs that an RC has been contacted and has taken ownership of the follow-up. It does not mark the Catalyst as retrained or change their compliance status in any way. Only a real, completed training event recorded in the data can do that.
